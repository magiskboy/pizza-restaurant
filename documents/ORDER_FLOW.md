# How to Place an Order

This guide walks you through the complete process of ordering from our pizza restaurant, from selecting items to receiving your order confirmation.

## Overview

Placing an order involves four simple steps:
1. **Add Items to Cart** - Browse the menu and add items you'd like to order
2. **Review Your Cart** - Check your order, adjust quantities, or remove items
3. **Checkout** - Provide your delivery information and complete your order
4. **Order Confirmation** - Receive confirmation that your order was placed successfully

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Add Items to Your Cart

**Where to find the menu:**
- On the home page, you'll see featured pizzas in the "Featured Pizzas" section
- Scroll down to see the "Our Full Menu" section with all available items
- Menu items are organized by categories: Pizza, Pasta, Salads, and Desserts

**How to add items:**
1. Browse through the menu items to see descriptions and prices
2. When you find something you want, click the **"Add to Order"** or **"Add"** button next to the item
3. You'll see a confirmation message saying "[Item Name] added to your order!"
4. If you add the same item again, the quantity will automatically increase

**Tips:**
- You can add multiple different items to your cart
- Adding the same item multiple times increases the quantity
- On mobile devices, you'll see a summary bar at the bottom of the screen showing how many items are in your cart

### Step 2: Review Your Cart

**How to view your cart:**
- Click on the cart icon in the header, or
- On mobile devices, click the "Checkout" button in the bottom summary bar

**What you'll see:**
- A list of all items you've added
- Each item shows:
  - Item name
  - Price per item
  - Current quantity
- Your order total at the bottom

**Managing your cart:**
- **Increase quantity**: Click the **+** button next to an item
- **Decrease quantity**: Click the **-** button next to an item
- **Remove an item**: Click the **"Remove"** button next to the item
- If you decrease quantity to zero, the item is automatically removed

**Your options:**
- **Continue Shopping**: Click this button to go back to the menu and add more items
- **Proceed to Checkout**: Click this button when you're ready to complete your order

**Empty cart:**
- If your cart is empty, you'll see a message saying "Your cart is empty"
- Click "Browse Menu" to return to the home page and start adding items

### Step 3: Checkout and Place Your Order

**What you'll see:**
The checkout page is divided into two sections:

**Left side - Order Summary:**
- A review of all items in your order
- Shows each item with its quantity and subtotal
- Displays your grand total at the bottom

**Right side - Delivery Information Form:**
- A form where you'll enter your delivery details

**Filling out the form:**
You'll need to provide the following information (all fields are required):

1. **Full Name** - Enter your first and last name
2. **Email** - Enter a valid email address where you'll receive order confirmation
3. **Phone** - Enter your phone number so we can contact you if needed
4. **Address** - Enter your street address for delivery
5. **City** - Enter your city
6. **ZIP Code** - Enter your postal/ZIP code

**Completing your order:**
1. Review your order summary on the left to make sure everything is correct
2. Fill in all the required delivery information fields
3. Double-check that your information is accurate
4. Click the **"Place Order"** button

**What happens next:**
- The system validates that all required fields are filled
- Your order is processed
- Your cart is cleared
- You're automatically taken to the order confirmation page

**Note:** If you try to checkout with an empty cart, you'll see a message and a button to return to the menu.

### Step 4: Order Confirmation

**What you'll see:**
After placing your order, you'll be taken to a confirmation page that shows:

- A green checkmark icon indicating success
- **"Order Placed Successfully!"** message
- A thank you message confirming we've received your order
- Order details including:
  - Your order number
  - Estimated delivery time (typically 20-30 minutes)
  - Your delivery address
  - A note that a confirmation email has been sent

**What you can do next:**
- **Back to Home**: Click to return to the home page
- **Order Again**: Click to start a new order

## Tips for a Smooth Ordering Experience

1. **Review before checkout**: Take a moment on the cart page to make sure you have everything you want
2. **Check quantities**: Make sure the quantities are correct before proceeding to checkout
3. **Have information ready**: Before checkout, have your delivery address and contact information ready
4. **Double-check your email**: Make sure you enter your email correctly to receive order confirmation
5. **Save your order number**: After placing your order, note your order number for reference

## Common Questions

**Can I modify my order after adding items?**
Yes! You can adjust quantities or remove items on the cart page before checkout.

**What if I accidentally add the wrong item?**
You can remove any item from your cart using the "Remove" button, or adjust the quantity to zero.

**Can I continue shopping after adding items?**
Yes! Click "Continue Shopping" on the cart page to go back and add more items.

**What if my cart is empty when I try to checkout?**
You'll see a message that your cart is empty and a button to return to the menu.

**Do I need to create an account to order?**
No, you can place an order without creating an account. Just provide your delivery information at checkout.

**How do I know my order was placed?**
You'll be taken to a confirmation page immediately after placing your order, and you'll receive a confirmation email.

**Can I place multiple orders?**
Yes! After completing an order, you can click "Order Again" to start a new order.

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during the ordering process:
- Make sure all required fields are filled in the checkout form
- Check that your email address is entered correctly
- Verify your delivery address is complete
- If problems persist, contact our customer service
